About BookClub


What are your favorite books? BookClub is the site where you can share what you've read with others and discover new books to read.

Your fellow BookClub members want to hear about the books you read and found interesting, as well as the ones you disliked and why.

Some tips to help you get started:

  • Check out trending books in the explore page.
  • Follow readers who have good taste in books for new recommendations.
  • Post reviews about books you liked or disliked and why (but leave out the spoilers!)
  • Help your fellow bookworms identify which book you are posting about by listing the title and author, and tagging the book with a #genre_tag.
Enjoy, and happy reading!
